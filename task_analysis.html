Task Analysis

Before we dived into the available data, we participated in an interview as well as a walking tour of Massachusetts Avenue, both led by Carol and Bob from Chester Square Neighbors. From these conversations, we learned about the rich history that the area holds, as well as the current challenges that the residents are facing. For our group specifically, their priorities lied in learning more about the usage and safety of the public transportation options.
